# Prosthetic Outreach Initiative - Volunteer Task Coordination

## Overview
This repository coordinates volunteer tasks across all teams for the **Prosthetic Outreach Initiative**, focused on bringing affordable prosthetic solutions to underserved communities worldwide.

## Mission
To coordinate volunteer efforts across research, engineering, clinical, and field deployment teams to make prosthetic care accessible and affordable.

## Teams & Roles

| Team | Role | Focus |
|------|------|-------|
| **Outreach Coordinators** | Chicago, Boston, Atlanta | Community engagement, event planning, stakeholder relations |
| **Clinical Research** | Medical directors, data collectors | Patient screening, data collection, compliance |
| **Engineering & Design** | CAD designers, 3D printing operators | Prosthetic design, fabrication, socket fitting |
| **Field Deployment** | Logistics, installers | On-site fitting, training, maintenance |
| **Fundraising & Grants** | Grant writers, donor relations | Funding acquisition, budgeting |

## Volunteer Task Tiers

### Tier 1 — Event Support

### Tier 3 — Technical

- Socket fabrication and fitting assistance
- Requires some technical training

### Tier 4 — Coordination
- Task assignment and scheduling
- Inter-team communication
- Progress tracking and reporting

### Tier 5 — Leadership
- Project management
- Strategic planning
- Stakeholder relations

## Key Clinical Trial Reference

| NCT ID | Title | Status | Sponsor | Location |
|--------|-------|--------|---------|----------|
| **NCT04725461** | Low Cost Socket for Lower Limb Amputees | COMPLETED | Shirley Ryan AbilityLab | Chicago, IL |

### NCT04725461 Summary
- **Focus**: Affordable prosthetic socket fabrication using expandable rigid foam
- **Intervention**: Low-cost lower limb prosthetic socket for transtibial amputees
- **Method**: 3D-printed outer shell filled with expanding polyurethane foam, forming a lightweight, comfortable socket in under 90 minutes
- **Significance**: Demonstrates that low-cost methods can produce prostheses with appropriate suspension and comfort for amputees in developing countries
- **Key Result**: Socket fabrication in 30 minutes (casting) and 90 minutes (completion), ideal for resource-limited settings

## Meeting Location for Coordinators

**Chicago, Boston, and Atlanta outreach coordinators** should meet at a central location. The geographic midpoint is in the Columbus, OH / Central Appalachia area. **Columbus, Ohio** is recommended as the meeting hub because:
- Central to all three cities (similar drive distance from each)
- Major city with full amenities (restaurants, hotels, conference facilities)
- Good airport connectivity for Boston and Atlanta coordinators
- Lower cost of hosting compared to coastal cities

**Recommended Venue Types:**
- **Cafes/Restaurants**: Columbus, OH downtown area
- **Conference Centers**: Greater Columbus Convention Center
- **Co-working Spaces**: Available throughout downtown
